# Fisher's Oysters Copy Brief

Fisher's Oysters is a family-run aquaculture business founded by Shaun Fisher, a proud Gorenpul man.

The business began during the 2023 oyster season with a bag of oysters, family support and a vision for the future. It grew through long days selling oysters at the West End Markets before purchasing the first oyster farm back home on Minjerribah (North Stradbroke Island).

Today, Fisher's Oysters operates three oyster farms on Minjerribah, growing native rock oysters and creating pathways into aquaculture, local work and on-water opportunities connected to Country and community.

## Website Focus

- Grow and supply native rock oysters from Quandamooka (Moreton Bay).
- Support training, employment and hands-on aquaculture experience on working oyster farms.
- Keep the copy separate from Quandamooka Oyster Farm Tours.
- Remove standalone tours, stories and five-principles content from the public site.
